How It Works
1. Create an Xvite
To create an xvite, simply enter the name of the event, all possible dates that work, and the earliest/latest times that the event can take place. Once you press 'Create Xvite', you will be provided a link to share with everyone in your group.
2. Fill out your availability
Before filling out the xvite, you first need to sign in with your name. Then, on the calendar of days and times, simply click and drag across all the times that work for you. Everyone will be able to mouse over times to see whether you're available or not.
3. Find times that work for everyone
As people add their availability, you will be able to view the times that others are available and which times overlap the most. The darker colors (pictured right) indicate that more people are free at those times. Pick the best time and send out the finalized event time!
